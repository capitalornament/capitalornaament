About CAPITAL ORNAMENT LTD

Capital Ornament Ltd is a leading interior design, painting, and POP company located at 151 Ida Plaza Solomon Lar Road, Jabi, Abuja, Nigeria. Our company is dedicated to creating exceptional designs, painting, and POP services that reflect the unique personality and style of our clients. Our interior design department is staffed with highly skilled and creative designers who specialize in creating beautiful and functional spaces. We work with our clients to understand their needs and preferences, and we use this information to create custom designs that are tailored to their individual tastes. Our interior designers are responsible for selecting and coordinating all of the elements that make up a space, including furniture, lighting, flooring, wall coverings, and accessories. They are trained to work with a wide range of styles, from traditional to contemporary, and they are experts at creating spaces that are both functional and aesthetically pleasing. Our painting department is staffed with experienced painters who are skilled at painting both interior and exterior surfaces. We use only the highest quality paints and materials, and we take the time to properly prepare surfaces before painting to ensure a smooth, even finish. Our painters are trained to work efficiently and neatly, and they take great care to protect our clients' property during the painting process. Our POP (Plaster of Paris) department is staffed with skilled artisans who specialize in creating beautiful and intricate designs using plaster. POP is a versatile material that can be used to create a wide range of decorative elements, including cornices, ceiling roses, columns, and arches. Our POP artisans are trained to work with a wide range of designs, from simple to ornate, and they take great care to ensure that each piece is crafted to perfection. At Capital Ornament Ltd, we understand that each of these services is important in creating a space that is truly exceptional. That's why we work closely with our clients to understand their needs and preferences, and we use our expertise to create designs that are both beautiful and functional. Whether you're looking to update the look of your home or office, or you're starting from scratch with a new build, we're here to help.
